Why We Exist

Real estate is a $400 trillion asset class running on 1960s infrastructure.

Access to institutional-grade real estate is gated — by minimum ticket size, by residency, by tax jurisdiction, by KYC. The vast majority of capable investors are locked out of the asset class that has preserved wealth across generations.

Fund-life models force exits at the worst moments in cycles. Quarterly NAV reports lag market by 90 days. The legal layer beneath tokenization wrappers is opaque. Holders bear the risk. Intermediaries capture most of the return.

Real estate isn’t broken because of bad UX or slow blockchains. It’s broken because the asset class lacks digital primitives — there’s no native way to use a property as collateral on-chain, no settlement layer for fractional transfer, no open data infrastructure for valuation.

GRES is being built as that primitive.

Not an iteration on RWA 1.0. A different architecture.

How We’re Building It

Decisions that shape the next 30 years.

Swiss-style Foundation Custody
100% of property deeds held by an independent Foundation modeled after Swiss law. Not by the protocol treasury. Not by founders. Why Swiss Foundation law provides 100+ year precedent for institutions designed to outlast their creators. Custody is structurally isolated from governance, founders cannot capture assets, and jurisdictional neutrality matters when underlying properties span multiple property law systems.
MiCA-Aligned Token Issuance
Token structure designed for European regulatory compliance from day one. GRES.ALPHA utility token; $GRES asset-backed token at Phase 04 mainnet. Why Institutional adoption requires regulatory clarity. MiCA passport is the path to scale, not a tax to delay.
AI-Augmented Valuation
Daily NAV computation via verifiable on-chain comparable analysis. Inputs are designed to be queryable; methodology is open; human governance reviews exceptions. Why Closes the information asymmetry between TradFi’s quarterly cycles and crypto’s 24/7 trading. NAV at the speed of the market, not the appraiser.
Perpetual Structure
No fund-life clock. No forced liquidation. No expiration date. Why Real estate is a 30-year asset class trapped in 5-10 year funds. Compounding requires time. Time requires the right structure.
BASE L2 Infrastructure
Built on Coinbase’s BASE for institutional gateway, low gas, and EVM compatibility. Why Bridges crypto-native operations with institutional onboarding paths. Real-world adoption requires real-world rails.
Open-Source Smart Contracts
Sale contract, token contract, future Oracle — all verified on Basescan, all auditable. Why Trust-minimization at the infrastructure layer. The code is the contract. The contract is public.
