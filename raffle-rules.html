Survey Prize Draw

Official Rules

No purchase necessary. These rules govern the prize draw open to participants of the GRES Protocol demand-validation survey.

Last updated · May 30, 2026
No purchase necessary to enter or win. A purchase or payment of any kind will not increase your chances of winning. Entry is completing the survey.

1. Sponsor

This prize draw (the "Draw") is administered by GRES Operator (the "Sponsor"), the for-profit entity behind the GRES Protocol. Questions may be sent to admin@gres.network.

2. Eligibility

  • Open to individuals who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.
  • Void where prohibited or restricted by law.
  • Employees of the Sponsor and their immediate family members are not eligible.

3. How to Enter

To enter, complete and submit the GRES Protocol survey, including a valid email address and the consent checkbox. Completing the survey is the only method of entry — no purchase, payment, or token acquisition is required or accepted as a means of entry.

  • One entry per person. Entries are de-duplicated by email address.
  • Entries that are incomplete, illegible, or submitted after the entry deadline are void.

4. Entry Period

The entry period opens upon publication of the survey and closes at 23:59 UTC on July 30, 2026. Entries received after this time will not be eligible.

5. The Prize

  • One (1) Apple iPhone 17 Pro Max.
  • The prize will be purchased online by the Sponsor and shipped to the winner. Model variant (storage/colour), delivery method, and shipping details will be arranged by email with the winner.
  • The prize is non-transferable and may not be exchanged for cash. The Sponsor reserves the right to substitute a prize of equal or greater value if the stated prize becomes unavailable.
  • Any applicable taxes, customs duties, or import fees are the sole responsibility of the winner.

6. Winner Selection & Notification

  • The winner will be selected at random from all valid entries on July 31, 2026.
  • The winner will be notified by email at the address provided in their entry.
  • The winner must respond within seven (7) days with the information needed to arrange delivery. If a selected winner does not respond within this period, the prize may be forfeited and an alternate winner drawn.

7. Data & Privacy

Information you submit is processed in accordance with the GRES Protocol Privacy Policy. Entry data is used solely to administer the Draw and, where you opt in, to notify you about the future launch of GRES$. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting admin@gres.network.

8. General

  • This Draw is in no way sponsored, endorsed, administered by, or associated with Apple Inc. Apple is not a sponsor of this Draw.
  • The Sponsor reserves the right to cancel, suspend, or modify the Draw if fraud, technical failure, or any factor beyond its reasonable control impairs its integrity.
  • The survey is informational only and is not an offer, solicitation, or investment advice. GRES$ is a future, asset-backed token (Phase 04) and is not yet available. Participation in the Draw confers no right to acquire any token or security.
